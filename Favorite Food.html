PIZZA RECIPE - TAWA PIZZA | PIZZA WITHOUT OVEN


This is the recipie for those who want to make pizza but don't want to use OVEN.

This recipe is the easiest recipe to make pizza

Ingredients

This recipe will produce 3 servings

  • 3 cups all purpose flour 300-315 gm
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instruction

  1. in a mixing bowl or pan take 3 cups all purpose flour, ½ teaspoon salt, ½ teaspoon sugar, ½ teaspoon baking soda and 1 teaspoon baking powder.
  2. mix very well
  3. add 2 tablespoons olive oil and 1 tablespoon lemon juice.
  4. add water in parts. begin to mix and then knead the dough
  5. keep on kneading till you get a soft and smooth dough. add water in parts. i added ½ cup+ 2 tablespoons water
  6. you have to really knead very well. knead at least for 10 to 12 minutes
  7. the dough should become elastic and stretchable. take a portion and stretch it. the dough should not break.
  8. heat a pan and brush or spread some olive oil on it
  9. now gently place the pizza on the pan
  10. when you see bubbles coming on it and the base has become golden, then flip.
  11. sprinkle grated pizza cheese or mozarella cheese. you can add cheese as required
  12. cover with a lid and let the veg pizza cook on a low flame or sim
  13. remove and serve veg pizza hot or warm with tomato ketchup. you can also sprinkle red chili flakes on top of the pizza, while serving

You can make maximum of 3 servings with this amount of ingredients
